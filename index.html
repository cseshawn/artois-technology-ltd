Questions

Frequently Asked

How do you structure your project engagements? +
We use an agile engagement model with clearly defined discovery, design, development, testing, and deployment phases. Clients receive weekly sprint updates, access to our project portal, and a dedicated account manager throughout.
What industries do you specialize in? +
We serve clients across fintech, healthcare, logistics, government, e-commerce, and enterprise SaaS. Our cross-industry experience means we bring best practices and proven patterns from adjacent domains into your project.
How do you ensure the security of client data? +
We are ISO 27001 certified and comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 Type II standards depending on client requirements. All development environments use encrypted data storage, strict access controls, and regular third-party penetration testing.
What is a typical project timeline? +
MVPs typically take 8–16 weeks. Enterprise platforms range from 4–12 months depending on complexity. We provide a detailed project roadmap after a discovery workshop, with milestones and deliverables clearly mapped out.
Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance? +
Yes — all projects include a 90-day hypercare support period post-launch. We also offer ongoing SLA-based support packages covering monitoring, incident response, performance optimization, and feature enhancements.
Can you work with our existing technology stack? +
Absolutely. Our engineers are polyglot and experienced with 50+ technologies. We can extend, integrate, or modernize your existing systems while preserving business continuity — no big-bang rewrites unless truly necessary.
